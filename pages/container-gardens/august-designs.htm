Container Potting Services

Choose the plants and we can do the potting: houseplants, hanging baskets, mixed containers, holiday center pieces, window boxes, shrubs and trees in containers.

Have a pot at home that needs a hole in it? Bring it in and for $1.00 we'll drill one for you (We cannot guarantee we won't break the pot in the process of drilling, but we'll do our best to not make that happen.)
