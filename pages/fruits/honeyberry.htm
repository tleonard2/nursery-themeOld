Native to Eastern Europe and Japan, this extremely hardy and adaptable member of the honeysuckle family (Lonicera kamchatica) produces delicious fruit with a flavor and color reminiscent of blueberries.

It is easy to grow and has very few disease or pest issues.

The dark blue, cylindrical fruits are thought to be high in antioxidants and beneficial phytochemicals.

This handsome shrub boasts fragrant white flowers in spring, followed by flavorful berries in early summer.

One of the only fruit bearing plants for part to full-shade! Plant two different varieties to increase yields.
