Currant varieties come in shades of red, black, and white.

Red currants are high in pectin, making them ideal for jams and jellies.

Black currants have five times the Vitamin C of oranges and make wonderful liqueurs.

White currants are typically sweeter and less acidic than red currants and are best eaten fresh.
