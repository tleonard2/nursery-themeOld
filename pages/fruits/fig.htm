Figs are not only a sweet, delicious summer treat, they are easy to grow and their beautiful foliage and form adds a tropical look to the garden.

Green, bronze and mahogany colored fruit with juicy amber or white flesh ripens during hot summer months. Ripe fruit will be soft to the touch and they will wilt on the stem. Many of our varieties produce a second crop of fruit in September, and all of the varieties we carry are self-fertile.

Fig trees are pest-free, adaptable to many climate and soil conditions, and drought tolerant once established. They can be pruned to stay small, espaliered onto a fence or hot wall, or allowed to sprawl.
