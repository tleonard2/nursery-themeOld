The month of May is a great time to start an edible hanging basket - and what a great gift it makes for Mother's Day!

Choose one centerpiece like bloody sorrel or basil, 3 fillers such as calendula, marigold or dianthus, 3 lettuce (Lollo rosso is used here) and 3 nasturtiums. Plant and give!
