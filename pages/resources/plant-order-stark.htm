Please follow these instructions.

Gift Cards

Please dial our main line (503-231-5050) if you wish to ONLY order a gift card. All other items may be submitted online.

Delivery

If you choose to get your order delivered, there will be a $30-$45 charge depending on your zip code.

Pickup

Please do not arrive at the store to make a pickup before you have received a phone call confirming your order and collecting payment.

Out of Stock?

If we are out of stock or don’t carry any of the items you have requested, and you have selected “NO substitutions” we will then send you an email canceling your order. (If you have not been contacted within 72 hours make sure to check your email). If you have selected “YES, I will take substitutions” then our staff will select plants and products as similar as possible to what you requested.

Need to cancel?

Please CALL the car-side line (503-231-5050, option 3) to cancel your order, be sure to provide the name of the person that submitted the order.

We thank you for your understanding as we develop this new department, and sincerely appreciate you trying out our new service! If It has been over 72 hours since you received an email confirming that your order was submitted and you have not received a phone call to collect payment, please pardon our error and call 503-231-5050, option 3.

Order Form Instructions

  1. Fill out ALL fields in the form.
  2. Please enter the products you would like to purchase. Be sure to include the size and number of each product.
  3. Add our phone number (503-231-5050) to your phone book (so that your phone recognizes our call).
  4. Wait for our call in 24-72 hours to confirm your order.
  5. Have your credit card available.
  6. If you have problems or questions, call us at 503-231-5050, option 3.
  7. PICK UP TIMES are between 10:00am and 5:00pm.
