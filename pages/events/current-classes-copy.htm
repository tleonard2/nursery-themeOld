with Whitney Bailey, Senior Conservationist at East Multnomah Soil and Water Conservation District

You will come away understanding how to: assess whether your site is suitable for a rain garden, determine the best location for it, calculate impervious surface and the size of your rain garden, choose appropriate plants, and maintain your garden for years of enjoyment.
