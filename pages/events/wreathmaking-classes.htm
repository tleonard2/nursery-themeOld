Online Wreath Making Classes

Join us for a virtual, holiday wreath making class! Each class will be taught by one of our excellent staff instructors, and by the time the class is over, you’ll see just how easy and fun it is to make a unique wreath that’s guaranteed to bring a bit of cheer into your home.

We do have prepackaged wreath making kits available at both locations for $22.99 that include 5 pounds of cut greens, a wreath ring, and paddle wire. We also have a full selection of bulk greens, cut flowers, branches, berries, and other add-ins to give your wreath a pop of color and a touch of magic.

If you would prefer not to shop for a wreath kit in person, you can use the Portland Nursery website to arrange for curbside pickup at the Division location.
