Open Wreath Making Classes & Private Group Wreath Making Workshops

We are sad to say that all in-person wreath making classes, both open and private, have been cancelled in accordance with new state COVID-19 guidelines. We are so sorry for any inconvenience this may cause. We’re looking into holding virtual wreath making classes, and this page will be updated with that information as soon as possible.

In the meantime, for those of you who’d love to make a wreath at home, we do have plenty of cut greens, wire, wreath rings, ribbons, and other sparkly wreath making supplies available at BOTH locations, and will continue to do so throughout the season.

As always, please be healthy, be safe, and grow your passions!
