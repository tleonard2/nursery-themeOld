Cider Tasting

Cider Tasting area organized by Wandering Aengus Ciderworks and features a number of producers.

As Cider has been thought of more and more as a “beer”, the original wine like heritage ciders have become harder to find. This will not be the case at the 2019 Apple Tasting where dry and tannin rich heritage ciders from Oregon and around the world will be available to sample and purchase. 21+ only.
