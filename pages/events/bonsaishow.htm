Every year in June, Portland Nursery on Division and the Bonsai Society of Portland (BSOP) invite the public and the members of BSOP to enter their bonsai for prizes in our annual show. Any customer can exhibit and just for entering, receives get a 10% off coupon.

All entries are judged by the public and the staff. Awards are presented to the top three customer entrants and the top three BSOP entrants:
