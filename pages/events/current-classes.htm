Gardening with Native Plants

with Linda Hardison and Dennis Albert of OregonFlora
Learn about native plants, what constitutes a “native,” and which species will grow well in your Willamette Valley garden or landscape. Learn how the design of your native plant garden can be informed by the resources of OregonFlora. OregonFlora, a program based at Oregon State University, provides comprehensive information about the native and naturalized plants of our state. Through the book, Flora of Oregon, the wildflower identification app, Facebook page, and the OregonFlora website, users can find photos, maps, and details about Oregon native plants suited to gardens, and where these can be purchased.

Firescaping

with Bonnie Bruce of Celilo Gardens
In the last few years it seems summers are getting hotter and fire “season” stretches out for longer.  And living in an urban environment is not always protection against a wildfire.
