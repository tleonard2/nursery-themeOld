Growing Fava Beans- Eat your cover crop!

with Marc Scheidecker
Legumes might well be a gardener’s best friend! September is a great month to plant some fava beans! These legumes “fix” nitrogen for our soil- for free!. In addition, the entire plant, which can grow to six feet, can be composted or turned under to add organic matter to the soil.

And some of today's varieties are DELICIOUS! We will discuss these topics as well as how to protect them from voracious black aphids.
