The Versatile Begonia

with Stephanie Willis of the American Begonia Society
Summer to Fall is the “show time” for many types of of begonias, from the stunning tuberous beauties, to the stately angel wing or cane types that have large clusters of flowers, to the wax begonias that are a durable mainstay of semi-shade plantings. Add in the exotic rex types with multi-colored leaves, and the rhizomatous with unusual leaf shapes, textures and colors and you have a family of plants to fill most gardeners warm weather landscape dreams. And they are easy to grow!

Stephanie Willis, a long time member of the American Begonia society, will give care tips for each of these types of begonias and show examples of rare terrarium types that can extend your begonia growing interest year round!

5050 SE STARK • Saturday • July 21 • 1:00pm
