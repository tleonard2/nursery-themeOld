January Online Gardening Classes

Sign up for one of our free, online classes below. All classes are hosted on Zoom, and Q/A portions of all lessons are being conducted through Zoom Chat. If you have any questions about how to register for a class, please email classes@portlandnursery.com

All classes are one hour unless otherwise noted.

Bottled Nature: Enclosed Terrarium Making with Peter Wilson

Learn to create your own miniature enclosed ecosystem. In this demonstration, Peter will go over the strategies behind choosing the right container and location for your terrarium and then will discuss the different kinds of flora and fauna to use. Finally, he will guide you through making your own Japanese-inspired mossy terrarium. You’ll be able to ask him any questions you might have about the terrarium making process using the chat function during the class.
Saturday • January 9 • 2:30pm
Click here to register for this class.

Repotting Houseplants: Tips and Demo with Jess Fancon

Are you perplexed by how to tell when it’s time to repot your houseplants? Jess will offer tips on how to tell when your plants are ready to move to bigger containers and what size pots to use. She will also demonstrate repotting techniques so you can help your indoor buddies stretch out in their new homes.
Saturday • January 16 • 11:30am
Click here to register for this class.

Medicinal Herbs for Immune Support with Missy Rohs, Arctos School of Herbal Studies

Want to give your immune system a boost this winter? Join community herbalist with Missy Rohs of Arctos School for an in-depth look at medicinal plants that can ward off colds and flus and keep you well through the season. We’ll cover easy to find herbs and talk about how best to use them, including dosage and contraindications. You’ll be surprised to find out that you already have some plant medicines in your kitchen!
Saturday • January 16 • 2:30pm
Click here to register for this class.

Seasons of Yoga: Winter Flow with Savannah Cyzick

All are welcome to join Savannah, a licensed yoga instructor with more than ten years of experience, for an hour of gentle yoga suited to this chilly time of year. Just like plants do during the winter, we can use this season as a time of restoration to prepare for the spring. This class incorporates simple flowing sequences to warm up the body as well as slower-paced movements to relax and rejuvenate our minds.
Saturday • January 23 • 1:00pm

Houseplant Basics with Ann Patterson

Learn how to pick the right plant for the different rooms in your home or office and get ideas for the best selections for your different indoor environments. Ann will explain low light vs. high light, watering and fertilizing tips, and how to troubleshoot common houseplant issues so you are more confident in greening up your indoor space.
Saturday • January 30 • 11:30am

February Online Gardening Classes

What to Do in the February Vegetable Garden with Robyn Streeter of Your Backyard Farmer

Join Robyn of Your Backyard Farmer for a great refresher on what to do in the veggie garden for the month of February. Topics covered in this virtual class will include seeding and planting cool season crops, preparing and improving your soils, and how to be best prepared for the spring!
