We’ve all had to learn to do things a little differently in 2020, and it’s with heavy hearts that we will be unable to host our traditional Apple Tasting festival onsite this fall.

We will, however, continue to support both our growers and community through apple sales and donations, and we’re planning to host several virtual events to bring you a taste of the festival we’ve happily hosted since 1987.

Apples will be available at both our Stark and Division locations for $.99/lb throughout the months of September and October, and we’ll be updating the varieties on hand right here along with some excellent tasting notes and advice from Ken—our resident Apple Expert!

Apples Available this week, September 7-13.

ELSTAR

Developed for European markets, this apple’s flavor varies from tart to sweet to balanced, depending on when it’s picked. Excellent for sauce or pies.

GINGER GOLDEN

Chance seedling from an orchard in West Virginia, this creamy yellow, sweet-tart apple is great for early fresh eating.

MCINTOSH

Origin 1800's, Canada. Sweet, tender, soft & juicy, especially good for dessert and cider.

GALA (only at 5050 SE Stark)

Yellow flesh is crisp and aromatic. Good for fresh eating.
