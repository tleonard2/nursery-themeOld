Kokedama Workshop

with Lucy Davenport of Bonsai Akira
Kokedama roughly translates from Japanese to mean “moss ball” – describing the wrapping of the plant’s root’s and soil in moss. It’s a beautiful and space-saving way to display houseplants, once artistically wrapped with string and hung from a ceiling or shelf, or set on a tray. Learn how to create one in this hands-on workshop and go home with tips on how to care for your new creation. $30 fee, payable at register day of class.
