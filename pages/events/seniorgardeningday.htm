Come join us at our 9000 Division Street location
Wednesday, September 18, 2018 1:00-3:00

Sponsors Home Instead, Portland Memory Garden and Earthtones Music Therapy Services partner with Portland Nursery to bring a variety of hands-on activities and special gardening demonstrations aimed at the elder gardeners of our community.

Special store discount for seniors to help celebrate! This free event happens rain or shine!

Individuals and families are always welcome! For groups of five or more, as well as those arriving in vans or buses, reservations are required at least two weeks in advance. For questions or to make a reservation, contact Jennifer Fifer: 503-490-1751 or jennifer.fifer@portlandhomeinstead.com.

Relax, visit with friends and enjoy refreshments. Pot up crocus bulbs, make lavender sachets and learn about fall container gardening.

Tables are set up under cover surrounded by beautiful and blooming displays.

Enjoy live music and there's always plenty of room to dance!
