2021 Seasonal Employment

Please note nearly all positions are seasonal, and must include weekends.

Spring at Portland Nursery is fast-paced and intensely busy. We are looking for people who can multitask like crazy and who are undaunted, if not downright inspired by an atmosphere of high energy and demands in their workplace. We seek individuals that are able to work as part of a team; we need a staff who can work together and stay calm, cheerful and enthusiastic through it all.

Applicants having plant knowledge is definitely a plus, but successful customer service and cashiering experience are of primary importance.

Seasonal Positions include:

  • Cashier/Customer Service
  • Production (off-loading, potting and stocking plants)
  • Loading Zone/Carry Out

Duties can include:

  • Cashiering
  • Answering multi-line phone and directing calls
  • Customer assistance in locating/selecting plant material, supplies, problem-solving
  • Maintenance of plant material and displays

Necessary Skills:

  • Ability to work well under pressure
  • Ability to work cooperatively
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Good communication skills
  • Ability to lift and carry fifty (50) pounds
  • Ability to stand for 2-3 hours at a time

If this sounds like you, come to the nursery and fill out an application, or download one here to complete and submit via fax, mail, or email to jobs@portlandnursery.com. You can also deliver the application to us at either of the locations noted on this page.
