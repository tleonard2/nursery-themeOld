Portland Nursery on Division

Portland Nursery at 5050 SE Stark

There is always something new to see: fresh perennials in bloom, the latest thing in garden art, enticing specialty conifers or the perfect houseplant for your home or office.

We love to share our enthusiasm for gardening with our customers as well as the community: We partner with over a hundred school and community-based gardening projects and programs annually, and contribute to nearly three hundred auctions and other fundraising events each year.

Around the circling of the seasons, we are always here – with plants and products and answers to your gardening questions – we hope to see you soon!

Portland Nursery at 9000 SE Division

Our Division Street location offers over 78,000 square feet of covered shopping, including a classroom for our weekly lectures and hands-on seminars, and a number of inspirational display gardens.

Our vast selection of exotic tropical houseplants and cacti will make you feel like you’ve entered a different hemisphere. And of course we offer all the plants, soils, pots and tools you’ll need to make your gardening dreams a reality.
