Native Ferns

We have a feature on native ferns that will help with addition of natives to your garden. Visit our Native Fern page for details. Native ferns are available year-round at the nursery.

Native ferns include: Sword Fern, Licorice Fern, Maidenhair Fern, Deer Fern, Giant Chain Fern and Lady Fern.
