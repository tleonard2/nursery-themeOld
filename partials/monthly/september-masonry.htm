Plant a Conifer
Add a conifer in September: the nights are cool and good for planting and we're fully stocked! Use our Conifer Guide to select the right tree for your garden.
Flowering Trees
Flowering trees add spring color and often a grand fall display of colorful leaves. You'll find them all on sale in September. See our tree page for some of our favorites.
Cool Season Vegetables
September is a good time for starting a fresh round of cool-season and overwintering crops. This includes lettuce, spinach, and other greens, as well as some cole crops such as broccoli and cauliflower. Short season root crops such as radishes can be grown now to harvest before winter, and overwintering roots such as garlic and onions can be planted. Just as importantly, its harvest time for those warm season crops! Visit our Vegetable Page for more information and September tips!
