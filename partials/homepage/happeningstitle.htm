What's Happening at Portland Nursery this {% partial 'resources/month' %}

This February, Portland Nursery has all of your early, spring favorites—from hellebore and daphne, to camellias and witch-hazel, or primrose and pansies. We’re also offering an outstanding selection of strawberry plants, blueberry bushes, and a growing assortment of roses and fruit trees. And if you’re not quite ready to plant in the ground, you can always explore our houseplant collection while you wait for the warmer days that are just around the corner.
